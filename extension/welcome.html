Styles

Write for the room

Different text needs different handling. Choose from four built-in styles — Grammar Only, Casual, Neutral, or Formal — or create your own with custom prompts, icons, and colours.

Each style carries its own thinking level so you get fast fixes when speed matters and deep precision when it counts.

Settings

Tuned to you

Auto-proofread on paste, trigger after a typing pause, enable the right-click context menu, switch themes — Proofreader adapts to the way you work. Everything is stored locally on your machine.

The side panel lives one click away in your browser toolbar. No app switching, no copying text into a chat window.

Languages

Seven languages, one panel

The interface is available in English, French, Spanish, Italian, German, Portuguese, and Dutch. Your browser language is detected automatically, or you can pick one manually.

The AI itself handles text in any language you throw at it — the UI just follows suit.

Get started in three steps

You'll be proofreading in under a minute.

1

Grab an API key

Grab a free API key from Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, or xAI — whichever you prefer.

2

Open the side panel

Click the Proofreader icon in your toolbar. Paste in your API key in settings and you're connected.

3

Paste and proofread

Drop any text into the editor, pick a style, and hit Proofread. Clean copy in seconds.
